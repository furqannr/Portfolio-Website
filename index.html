SurgiAI

AI Powered Solution

Pre-surgery, it gathers and organizes relevant information to prepare a comprehensive report for surgical preparation. During surgery, it responds to voice commands from the surgical team, providing instant access to patient history or real-time consultation on specific surgical procedures. Thesis Lens

AI Powered Solution

Thesis Lens is an AI-powered application that simplifies the thesis review process, ensuring formatting and content accuracy to promote academic excellence. By providing students with timely, automated feedback, it reduces stress and allows them to focus on their research. For professors, it saves time by automating repetitive tasks, enabling more focus on mentoring. Additionally, Thesis Lens minimizes paper waste by reducing the need for printed drafts, offering an eco-friendly solution that supports both mental well-being and a greener academic environment.
